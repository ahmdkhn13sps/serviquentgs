About the Company

One Parent Brand.
Multiple Specialist Divisions.

We operate as Serviquent Global Solutions — the BPO and IT outsourcing arm of Serviquent Prime Solutions. Our parent brand is a global professional services group trusted by hundreds of enterprises across 40+ countries spanning telecom infrastructure engineering, business process outsourcing and managed IT.

Founded on the belief that every business deserves access to world-class operational talent, we have grown into a trusted partner for Fortune 500 companies, fast-scaling startups, ISPs, utilities, financial institutions and healthcare providers worldwide.

This division focuses exclusively on BPO operations, IT managed services, debt recovery and intelligent process automation — combining the Serviquent brand's reputation with deep domain expertise, certified delivery centers and AI-powered workflows built to deliver real, measurable outcomes.
